Dubai Real Estate for Brokers & Investors
Channel ID: YTCR
Educational and lifestyle YouTube channel focused on Dubai real estate for high-performing brokers and international property investors. Founded to support Core Realty — an international club of elite real estate brokers operating since 2022 under the license of INTRUST Real Estate L.L.C. Serves as a brand awareness engine, broker recruitment funnel, and trust-building platform for investors exploring the Dubai property market.
👥Target Audience
Geography

USA, Europe (UK, Germany, France, CIS countries), Asia, Australia — English-speaking investors and real estate professionals worldwide

Who Watches
  • Experienced real estate brokers earning 500,000+ AED/year considering relocation to Dubai
  • International property investors evaluating Dubai as a portfolio diversification market
  • High-net-worth individuals seeking Golden Visa through real estate investment
  • Real estate professionals exploring AI-powered brokerage tools and CRM systems
  • Entrepreneurs and digital nomads interested in Dubai's tax-free income environment
Pain Points
"How do I start as a real estate broker in Dubai?"

"Which areas in Dubai give the best ROI?"
Off-plan vs ready, rental yields, capital appreciation
"How do I join an elite brokerage network?"
Commission splits, support systems, what to look for
"What tech tools do top brokers use?"
AI ERP, lead management, call tracking, CRM platforms

Market cycles, oversupply myths, regulatory changes

AED 2M threshold, requirements, timeline
"What mistakes do new brokers make in Dubai?"
Common pitfalls, client management, legal compliance
🎬Content Format
Video Types
  • Expert interviews (primary format)
  • Property tours & walkthroughs
  • Market analysis & data breakdowns
  • Broker lifestyle vlogs
  • Shorts: tips, myths, quick insights (1-3 min)
Parameters
  • Duration: 45-75 min (interviews), 10-20 min (market updates), 5-10 min (shorts)
  • Speakers: 2 (host + guest) for interviews; 1 (host) for tours
  • Language: EN
🎨Style & Tone
  • Delivery: Expert, aspirational, transparent — showcase real broker success stories, not sales pitches
  • Editing pace: Dynamic — mix of talking head, property b-roll, data overlays, and on-location footage
  • B-Roll: Dubai skyline, luxury property walkthroughs, office tours (Core Realty coworking space & photo zones), community events (brunches, award ceremonies, after-parties)
