aboutAbout This Guideline

Core Realty Dubai — educational and lifestyle YouTube channel focused on Dubai real estate for high-performing brokers and international property investors. Founded to support Core Realty — an international club of elite real estate brokers operating since 2022 under the license of INTRUST Real Estate L.L.C.

This catalog defines 20 screen types in 6 categories · 2 fonts (Playfair Display + Inter) · 10 colors. It serves as the Pre-Edit visual system for YTCR video production, ensuring consistency across all videos on the channel.
